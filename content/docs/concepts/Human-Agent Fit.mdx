# 인간-에이전트 핏

The long-term goal is not generic intelligence improvement but better fit between the user's evolving needs and the agent's execution style, judgment defaults, and note-derived operating memory.

## What improves fit
- Better extraction of stable preferences
- Better comparison between user-written revisions and agent-maintained versions
- Better detection of drift and mismatch
- Better anticipation of task shape and desired output
